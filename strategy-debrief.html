2

Find the Gaps

Use /infogain to calculate what competitors are missing

What Happens

For each target query (e.g., "CSAT survey template"), the /infogain agent:

  • Fetches the top 10 ranking pages
  • Extracts every claim, statistic, and source they cite
  • Builds a "coverage matrix" showing which claims appear in how many competitors
  • Calculates a redundancy score (we target <70% overlap)
  • Identifies "nuggets" (atomic information needs) with 0-2 competitor coverage

Why This Works

This is based on Google's own patent. If 8 out of 10 competitors already explain "what CSAT is", covering that adds near-zero value. But if only 1 competitor talks about "scale selection science" (3-point vs 5-point), that's a high-gain nugget worth prioritizing.

The Output

A prioritized list of sections to build, each addressing a gap in competitor coverage. We know exactly what NOT to waste time on (saturated nuggets) and what WILL move the needle (0-2 coverage gaps).
